What the marks mean
[oo] Accept — the source supports the claim.
[o ] Caution — it supports the claim, but indirectly or partially.
[r ] Needs review — not enough evidence was checked, or the raters disagree.
[d ] Reject — the source does not support, or contradicts, the claim.
[··] Pending — not yet rated/decided.
Unsupported means the source appears not to support the claim; unverified / needs review means CiteVahti could not check enough evidence; contradicts means it argues against it.
CiteVahti checks whether a claim is supported by its source — not whether the claim is true or clinically valid.
