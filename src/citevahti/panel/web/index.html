Manuscript marks — a claim's status
[oo] Accepted — has an accepted, supporting citation.
[o ] Needs support — no accepted supporting evidence yet.
[r ] Review needed — raters disagree, or it's flagged for a second review.
[d ] Decided — every candidate settled, none accepted.
[u ] Untestable — the cited source is outside the indexed literature.
[··] Pending — not yet rated/decided.
In the review card you pick a decision — Accept · Caution · Needs review · Reject — and that sets the claim's mark above.
CiteVahti checks whether a claim is supported by its source — not whether the claim is true or clinically valid.
Keyboard shortcuts

1–7 pick a support rating (while rating)

Shortcuts never fire while you're typing in a box. Writing a lot? — your typing is never affected either way.
