CiteVahti · a product of Vahtian

Verify the claim before you cite it.

CiteVahti is citation-integrity infrastructure. Every manuscript claim is tested against its candidate papers through a blinded human → AI → adjudication workflow, and a reference enters Zotero only as the audited, undoable last step of a human decision.

What it does

  • The claim is the spine. Candidate papers hang off each manuscript claim — not the other way around.
  • Blinded second rating. The AI rates support independently and stays hidden until you rate; disagreements route to you.
  • The human is always the decider. AI values are advisory, never decisive, never silently propagated.
  • Decision-gated, undoable Zotero write. Preview → confirm → write → undo, with fail-closed dedupe — never a silent or fabricated citation.
  • Hash-chained audit trail. The reasoning is recorded, so the workflow can be reported transparently in a methods section.

FAQ

  • What is CiteVahti? A citation-integrity tool that tests every claim in a manuscript against its candidate papers through a blinded human → AI → adjudication workflow, then writes verified references to Zotero in a decision-gated, undoable step.
  • Is it free? Yes — the open core is Apache-2.0, free, and local-first for researchers. Vahtian sells paid infrastructure to organizations that need auditable citation integrity at publication scale.
  • How is it different from a reference manager? A reference manager stores and formats citations; CiteVahti verifies them — checking whether each claim is actually supported by the paper cited for it before anything is written.
  • Does it send my manuscript to the cloud? No. Local-first, single-user, no telemetry. The only outbound calls are literature lookups (PubMed, OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, Crossref) and, if you connect it, your Zotero.
